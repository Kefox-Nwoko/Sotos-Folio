About Me

Now acquainted with my identity, allow me to elaborate further. I am a certified proofreading and editing professional hailing from the UK, with a specialized focus on academic publications. Complementing this expertise is my background in graphic design, fortified by a degree, which imbues my editing endeavors with an aesthetic finesse. Leveraging this duality, I am proficient in selecting visually pleasing fonts tailored to your manuscript, book, journal, magazine, or web content.

Think of it as a synergy. My background in graphic design seamlessly integrates with the editing process, whether it's selecting fonts for your publications or crafting a captivating book cover design. I can cater to all your needs.
